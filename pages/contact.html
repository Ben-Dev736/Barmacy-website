Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a prescription?
Simply go to our Prescriptions page, fill in your details, and upload a clear photo of your prescription. Our pharmacists will review it within 2 hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), Credit/Debit Cards, and Cash on Delivery within Kigali.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Express delivery (same day) is available for orders placed before 12 PM in Kigali.
Are all medicines genuine?
Yes! We source all medicines from licensed distributors and manufacturers. Every product is 100% authentic and verified.
Can I return medicines?
Due to safety regulations, medicines cannot be returned once sold. However, if you receive damaged products, contact us within 24 hours.
Do you offer prescription refills?
Yes! We offer automatic refill reminders for chronic medications. Contact our support to set up recurring orders.
